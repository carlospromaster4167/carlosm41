About This Site

This website is created for fans of the Boston Celtics who want to explore player information, team identity, and interactive features across multiple pages.

Team Focus

The Celtics emphasize defense, ball movement, and unselfish plays. Every player on the roster has a defined role that contributes to team success throughout the season.

This page introduces the core players and explains how they fit into the overall system before diving deeper on later pages.
